About Me

I am a loving and gentle person who is willing and always ready to contribute positively to humanity through working with like-minded individuals. I am a graduate of Adekunle Ajasin University Akungba Akoko from the department of History and International Studies. As someone who is always ready to contribute to humanity I have served in different organizations. For instance I once serve as the president National Association of Oyin Students in the year 2021. During this period I together with my team organized programs that have positive impacts on the life of both the old and young in the community, during this time there was the first of it kind medical outreach organized by the student union body,there was also skill acquisition program for the students in the community.
